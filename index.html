Machu Picchu

Machu Picchu is located more than 7,000 feet above sea level in the Andes Mountains in Peru and is the most popular tourist destination in Peru. It is a symbol of the Incan Enpire and was built around 1450AD. In 2007 it was named as one of the Seven Wonders of the World.

The Northern Lights

The Northern Lights, also called Aurora Borealis is a natural phenomenon consisting of light displays across the night sky. It is mostly visible at high elevations and can be seen by the naked eye.
